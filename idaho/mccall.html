McCall

McCall Idaho is one of the best kept secrets in all of Idaho. McCall is located just 90 miles north of the state capital Boise. The town is situated on the southern shore of Payette Lake. The most popular time of year for McCall would be the summer where boating on Payette Lake is highly popular, you can also golf at one of five golf courses in the area, and live music can be found almost nightly. The winter time is also very active for McCall, as snow skiing and snow mobiling are very popular this time of year.
