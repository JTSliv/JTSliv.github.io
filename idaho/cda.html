Couer D'Alene

Couer D'Alene is a great golf destination with 5 courses within the city limits and eight more within a 20 mile radius. Lake Couer D'Alene is also home to many water sports that are highly popular during the summer time. The area is also home to great skiing with mountains such as Lookout Pass, Silver Mountain, and Schweitzer all within a short distance.
