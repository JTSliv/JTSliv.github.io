Bear Lake

I am lucky to call Bear Lake my home and where I grew up. Bear Lake offers a small town atmosphere that lets you escape the fast lifestyle of nearby Salt Lake City and other major cities. Bear Lake is popular among tourists for it's crystal clear and cooling water. Many people enjoy boating, jetskiing, and fishing on the lake during the summertime. Golfing and hiking are also highly popular in the surrounding areas. Bear Lake is also home to nearby Beaver Mountain Ski Resort which offers plenty of variety for beginner, intermediate, and advanced skiers.
